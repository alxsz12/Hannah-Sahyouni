Hi, I'm Hannah

It's my joy and passion to care for new moms and their precious newborns. It's my goal to help, educate, and empower women and new parents to be confident in their ability to care well for their newborns. I do this by listening, answering questions, and teaching basic newborn care in the comfort of your home

My Services

FREE CONSULTATION
Get to know me! Typically consultation occurs before baby is born. Ask me anything, and figure out if I'd be a good fit for what you're looking for. Help me to know what types of things you are nervous about.
OVERNIGHT NEWBORN CARE
Get some rest in the first couple of weeks! When I get there, you can just go to bed! I will handle all things baby overnight for you, and a chore or two as well. Timing: 9pm-7am.
DAYTIME POSTPARTUM VISIT
3 hour visit during daytime hours. How are things going? What is going well? What could be going better? Let me show you some helpful tips. And you can go ahead and take a nap after!
BABY CARE CLASSES
Let me teach you the basics! This is a private, tailored to you class on newborn care and the basics of having a baby at home.
